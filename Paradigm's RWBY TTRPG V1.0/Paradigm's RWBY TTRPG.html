ATTRIBUTES
STR
Strength
Your ability to perform physical tasks when trying to physically exert and overpower an obstacle uses Strength. Strength is one of the primary attributes used for those who possess great melee prowess.
END
Endurance
Your ability to take punishment after your Aura has been broken and how long you can perform any task that requires physical exertion. Withstanding hazardous environmental and physical trauma is also a key aspect of those with high Endurance. Additionally, Endurance determines a Character’s Health Points.
AGI
Agility
Your ability to move quickly, precisely, and with poise. Tasks that require accurate and precise movement or use of delicate maneuvers require Agility. Agility is one of the primary attributes used by those excelling in ranged combat. It also affects your movement bonus.
PER
Perception
Your ability to defend yourself relies on your Perception. Perception is used to spot weaknesses or incoming attacks to better defend oneself as well as hone in on your own intuition about others.
WIL
Willpower
Your ability to Influence others and manipulate your Semblance. You also have a knack for understanding how to approach aggressive and delicate social interactions.
DIS
Discipline
Your ability to be studious and keep calm during the heat of battle. Characters with high Discipline exhibit extreme form and finesse when wielding Dust. Individuals with high Discipline are highly skilled with Dust without applying it to a weapon.
MOVE
Movement
The amount of distance you can travel with one Action.
The default distance you move is 15ft. Gain +5ft for every 4 AGI.

RESOURCES
HP
HP is equal to your END + 1. How much damage a Character can withstand without their Aura before becoming Defeated. Health Points are commonly referred to as HP. When your HP is 0, your Character is considered Defeated. Only HP restoration can bring a Character out of the Defeated state.
/ 
AURA
Aura is equal to your WIL + DIS + 2. The physical manifestation of a Character’s soul. Every Huntsman and Huntress is trained in its capabilities. Aura acts as a barrier to protect your body from damage. When your Aura is 0 your Character cannot use their Semblance; any damage inflicted will be directed to your HP.
/ 
CAPACITY
The amount of ammunition left in a magazine, emergency supplies, or easily accessible Dust a character has at the current moment. Capacity is expended when a Character performs a ranged attack, Dust Skill Check or other Combat Action. When your Capacity is 0 your Character cannot perform Actions that require Capacity to be performed. Capacity may be restored to its maximum by using a Minor Action called Reload.
/ 
ROC
A special resource available in Combat Encounters. Rule of Cool may be awarded by GM for a lavish and cool description of an attack, a partner attack, team attack, or anything at all they feel deserves it. The maximum RoC you can possess is 5.
/ 
DESTINY
A special Resource that is always available. It allows a re-roll of any die in the game!
ARMOR
Some Characters wear extra Armor, or a Faunus may have natural Armor. Reduce any damage that would be directed to your HP by 1 for every point of Armor you possess to a minimum of 1. The maximum Armor you may ever have is equal to 1 + 1 for every 4 points of Strength you possess.
/ 
BARRIER
An augmentation to your Aura is Barrier. The maximum Barrier you may possess is equal to your Level/2 + 1.
/ 
SUPPLY
Representing the amount of backup ammunition, Dust, or other essentials the Character has on their person but not readily available. Supplies are expended by 1 when a Character performs a Reload. When your Supplies are 0 your Character cannot perform a Reload.

SKILL CHECKS
Carry
Uses your STR and END Attributes. The ability to carry large or heavy objects without tiring or collapsing.
Deceit
Uses your AGI and WIL Attributes. You have a knack for concealing away your true intentions, information and your own body language to make yourself hard to read.
Dust
Uses your DIS Attribute and Level / 2. Harnessing the power of Dust, you attempt to bend the environment to your will, adding or molding it in a manner you see fit.
Endure
Uses your END Attribute and Level / 2. Grrr! Being able to tough out pain to your body whether it is external or self induced from exertion.
Engineering
Uses your AGI Attribute and Level / 2. Careful! Working on simple or complex equipment takes a careful hand.
Expertise
Uses your DIS and WIL Attributes. You have extensive knowledge in one or more particular fields that you have read or studied in some manner.
First Aid
Uses your PER and END Attributes. Every second counts! You are able to identify and understand the limits of the physical body’s tolerances. This allows you to provide medical attention, stabalize an individual or able to assess what an individual is specifically suffering from.
Force
Uses your STR Attribute and Level / 2. Brute force! Use your Character’s might to attempt to destroy or remove an obstacle in your path that is preventing you from moving forward.
Grapple
Uses your STR and PER Attributes. Use a keen eye and strength to keep hold of your target.
Intimidate
Uses your STR and WIL Attributes. Attempting to use impending force or tone of voice to force another individual to be persuaded or give up assets.
Intuition
Uses your PER Attribute and Level / 2. It’s quiet…too quiet. Through careful study of another individual’s body language, speech or other actions, you are able to determine whether a situation feels off or if someone may be deceitful of their intentions.
Investigate
Uses your PER and DIS Attributes. What’s that? Careful observation and checking of a particular item, indivdual or location may reveal more than what is on the surface.
Negotiate
Uses your WIL and PER Attributes. Taking in the atmosphere and feeling of the situation, you are able to attempt to negotiate and diffuse a situation or convince someone of an alternative.
Performance
Uses your END and DIS Attributes. Putting your expertise to work is tough! No matter how long you are showing off to your friends or allies with your skills, it can take a toll on you.
Reflex
Uses your AGI and PER Attributes. Uh oh! Your body’s natural ability to react at a moment’s notice to danger to move out of harms way or manuever with caution both horizontally and vertically.
Resolve
Uses your END and WIL Attributes. Mind over matter! You are able to steel your nerves and mind over a long period of time against negative influences that may otherwise break other individuals.
Semblance
Uses your WIL Attribute and Level / 2. Your mind is calm and clear, focusing for a split moment on using the gift you possess. Your semblance comes to power or has always been there.
Stealth
Uses your AGI and DIS Attributes. Shhh! You make adjustments while moving quickly, making sure you are unheard and unoticed with the utmost care and precision of movement through practice.

ACTIVE DUST
Black
Black (Light)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Bewildered.
Bewildered: Decrease Attacks by 2 until the end of the next Combat Round. If Bewildered would be applied while an Entity is already Bewildered, upgrade Bewildered with Blinded. If an Entity is Blinded, Bewildered does not apply.
Blinded: Decrease Attacks by 4 until the end of the next Combat Round.

Support: Gain 1 point of Barrier equal to the Action utilizing Black Dust / 10.
Blue
Blue (Water)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Fumbled.
Fumbled: You may not perform Boost, Dust Swap, Reload, and Weapon Transformation until the end of the next Combat Round. If Fumbled would be applied a second time while an Entity is already Fumbled, also gain Disarmed.
Disarmed: You may not perform an Attack with 1 weapon until the end of the next Combat Round.

Support: Gain Regeneration.
Regeneration: Restore 1 HP at the end of your Turn. Lose Regeneration when your HP reaches half or more of your maximum HP.
Brown
Brown (Acid)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful reduce Armor by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter. If your Target does not have Armor, inflict Damage equal to the Action that utilized Brown Dust / 10.

Support: Remove 1 Detrimental Status Effect except Disarmed, Grappled, Fumbled, Poisoned, or Toxified.
Green
Green (Wind)

Offense: Attacks and Dust Skill Checks are performed at 1 range increment higher. Your Actions performed as Attacks increase their Attack equal to the Action that utilized Green Dust / 10.

Support: Gain Light Footed.
Light Footed: Increase Movement by 10ft. You may increase or decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1 when Light Footed is applied. Lose Light Footed after performing a Move Action.
Orange
Orange (Lightning)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful bounce. Inflict Damage equal to the Action that utilized Orange Dust / 10 to an Entity within Close range of your Target. You may perform this Damage again using the previous Entity selected as the new origin point for determining a new Target to inflict this Damage to.

Support: Gain Charged.
Charged: You may perform a free Boost with any Attack, Defense Check, or Skill Check even if Boost couldn’t be used normally. Lose Charged after this effect has resolved.
Pink
Pink (Sonic)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Disoriented.
Disoriented: Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Checks by 2 until the end of the next Combat Round. Empower cannot be used. If Disoriented would be applied while an Entity is already Disoriented, upgrade Disoriented with Concussed. If an Entity is Concussed, Disoriented does not apply.
Concussed: Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Checks by 4 until the end of the next Combat Round. Empower cannot be used.

Support: Gain Harmonized.
Harmonized: Your next Empower is performed with Advantage. Lose Harmonized after this effect has resolved.
Red
Red (Fire)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Burning to your Target.
Burning: Inflict Damage equal to the Action that inflicted Burning / 10 at the end of your Turn. If Burning would be applied while an Entity is already Burning, increase the Burning Damage by using the previous formula.

Support: Gain Extinguish.
Extinguish: Nullify the next Detrimental Status Effect that would be applied. Lose Extinguish after this effect has resolved.
Violet
Violet (Force)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful knock back your Target up to 15ft. If your Target would impact another Entity or solid structure, Inflict Damage equal to the Action that utilized Violet Dust / 10 to your Target and any Entity they collide into.

Support: Immediately move up to 15ft in any Direction ignoring any Detrimental Status Effects.
White
White (Ice)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Chilled.
Chilled: Move and Capacity Move distance is reduced by 5ft until the end of the next Combat Round. Decrease your Initiative order by 1 when Chilled is inflicted. If Chilled would be applied while an Entity is already Chilled, upgrade Chilled with Frostbitten. If an Entity is Frostbitten, Chilled does not apply.
Frostbitten: Move and Capacity Move distance is reduced by 10ft until the end of the next Combat Round. Decrease your Initiative order by 2 when Frostbitten is inflicted.

Support: Gain Diffraction.
Diffraction: The next Damage greater than 1 inflicted to you is halved before performing any other Damage reduction effects. Lose Diffraction after this effect has resolved.
Yellow
Yellow (Earth)

Offense: Your Attacks and Dust Checks that are successful inflict Weakened.
Weakened: Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Checks by 2 until the end of the next Combat Round. Persevere cannot be used. If Weakened would be applied while an Entity is already Weakened, upgrade Weakened with Exhausted. If an Entity is Exhausted, Weakened does not apply.
Exhausted: Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Checks by 4 until the end of the next Combat Round. Persevere cannot be used.

Support: Gain 1 point of Armor.
SIGNATURE ACTION
Signature Action
Your custom Signature Action that is unique to your character! You may perform a Signature Action once per Combat Encounter.
Signature Type
Signature Action Type utilizes your Strength for Melee, Agility for Ranged, and Discipline for Characters with the Dust Weaver Specialization.
RoC Unlock
The amount of RoC expended to perform this Signature Action.
Signature Attribute
The secondary Attribute that is utilized while performing your Signature Action.
Area of Effect
While calculating Total Damage with your Signature Action, inflict half of your Total Damage to all Adversaries in Close range of your Target. If you are utilizing the High Explosive, Collateral Damage, or Cleaving Specializations, perform your Signature Action Damage with Advantage instead of the prior effect. If you elect to utilize your Signature Action in a Team Oriented manner, the Target Ally of your Signature Action and all Close range Allies are affected by your other Signature Effect(s).
Beneficial Status
Critical Threshold
Reduce the Critical Threshold of your Target by 5 while performing your Signature Action.
Defense Bypass
Ignore the Barrier and Armor of your Target while performing your Signature Action.
Detrimental Status
Disrupt
While Targeting 1 or more Entities, you may move them up to 15ft in any direction and decrease their position by 1 in the Initiative Order with your successful Signature Action.
Dust Bolster
You always apply the strongest Status condition if applicable from any Dust applied to your Signature Action. Add +5 to your Signature Action for the purposes of calculating any Dust inflicted Status Effect that inflicts Damage when dividing by 10.
Initiative Change
Increase or Decrease your position in the Initiative Order up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action at the end of the Combat Round.
Multi Target
You may Target Entities in range of you up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action.
Outmaneuver
Immediately perform a free Move Action with a Capacity Move.
Range
Increase the range of your Signature Action or another Action during the current Combat Round up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action.
Recycle
Restore Capacity equal to your Signature Action / 10. Capacity restored in this manner cannot exceed your Maximum Capacity.
Siphon
Restore Aura equal to your Signature Action / 10. Aura restored in this manner cannot exceed your Maximum Aura.
Teamwork
Target may immediately perform 1 Action. This Action utilizes their current Combat Round’s Action Pool.
